My design process

From concept to launch to continuous improvement using different design and research methodologies.

  • 1. Research

    Understand users, business needs, metrics, market trends and competition.

  • 2. Design

    Create user journeys, flowcharts, prototypes and scalable design systems.

  • 3. Test

    Conduct usability tests, A/B testing and performance evaluations.

  • 4. Define

    Iterate, set a roadmap and collaborate with developers for seamless hand-offs.
